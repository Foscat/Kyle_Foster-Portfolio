# Style Checklist

## Grammar

- Subject-verb agreement is correct.
- Pronoun references are unambiguous.
- Punctuation supports readability.

## Clarity

- Main ask appears early.
- Sentences are concrete and direct.
- Paragraphs each have one core idea.

## Tone

- Matches requested voice (formal/friendly/direct).
- Avoids passive-aggressive phrasing.
- Ends with clear next step when action is needed.
